The Breakfast Frequency

By Brian Foo

There are many meals of the day I share with friends: brunches, lunches, dinners, and desserts. Our breakfasts, on the other hand, are embedded in private daily rituals. As an occasional break from news or music, wouldn't it be nice to have a radio where we can occasionally tune into each others' morning breakfast sounds? Not to snoop or scrutinize but to feel a friendly, mostly silent, presence while we prepare for the day and remind ourselves that, between social gatherings and before we put on our public face, we all participate in the sometimes-overlapping mundane but necessary morning routines.
